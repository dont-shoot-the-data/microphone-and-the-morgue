# The Microphone and the Morgue

**[Read it live](https://dont-shoot-the-data.github.io/microphone-and-the-morgue/)**

Every June, California's Attorney General holds a press conference for the *Hate Crime in California* report and lets the *Crime in California* report out quietly, days later, with no event. One report names a victim. The other names a suspect. This piece reads both, using nothing but his own numbers.

## What it covers

- The hate crime report he stages, where six murders and ten rapes across four years sit inside a category that is overwhelmingly vandalism and threats.
- The homicide and arrest data he files without a word, broken down by race, crime by crime.
- Who actually kills whom in California, drawn from the FBI's own homicide files.
- A year-by-year record of the rollout, and a standing prediction for the next cycle you can check against the reports as they drop.

## Verify it yourself

Every figure links to its source. Two buttons in the piece open the full run of official PDFs: 25 years of *Crime in California* behind the arrest data, and 4 years of *Hate Crime in California* behind the composition numbers. Nothing here asks to be taken on faith.

## Sources

- FBI Supplementary Homicide Reports, via the Murder Accountability Project.
